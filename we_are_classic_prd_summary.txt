WE ARE CLASSIC - PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (SUMMARY)

=============================================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
=============================================================================

Project Name: We Are Classic NGO Landing Page
Project Type: Marketing Website / Landing Page
Target Launch: Q1 2025

=============================================================================
BUSINESS GOALS
=============================================================================

Primary Goal:
- Attract and convert Corporate Sponsors (CSR Managers)
- Attract and engage Professional Artists and Orchestra Directors

Secondary Goals:
- Build newsletter subscriber base
- Establish brand presence and credibility
- Communicate mission and impact effectively

Critical Success Metrics:
- Contact form submissions (Sponsors & Artists)
- Newsletter subscription rate
- Time on page and engagement metrics
- Geographic reach (Romania focus)

=============================================================================
TARGET AUDIENCE
=============================================================================

Primary Audience 1: Corporate Sponsors / CSR Managers
- Demographics: 35-55 years old, C-level executives, CSR/Marketing managers
- Pain Points: Finding meaningful CSR partnerships, demonstrating social impact
- Motivations: Corporate social responsibility, brand alignment, tax benefits
- Key Message: "Support the next generation of classical musicians"

Primary Audience 2: Professional Artists / Orchestra Directors
- Demographics: 25-65 years old, professional musicians, music educators
- Pain Points: Limited funding, lack of performance opportunities, resource constraints
- Motivations: Career advancement, artistic expression, community impact
- Key Message: "Join a community that values and supports your artistry"

Secondary Audience: General Public
- Music enthusiasts, parents of young musicians, community members
- Goal: Newsletter subscriptions, event attendance, word-of-mouth marketing

=============================================================================
KEY SECTIONS & CONTENT
=============================================================================

1. HERO SECTION
   - Full-width video background (orchestra performance)
   - Oversized headline in GFS Didot
   - Primary CTA: "Contact Us"
   - Secondary action: Scroll to learn more

2. VISION SECTION (#vision)
   - Title: "Our Vision"
   - Subtitle: "A World United by Classical Music"
   - Three Pillars:
     a) Education - Youth music programs
     b) Accessibility - Breaking down barriers
     c) Excellence - Supporting world-class performances
   - Visual: Icons or illustrations representing each pillar

3. OFFERINGS SECTION (#offerings)
   - Title: "What We Offer"
   - Programs:
     a) Youth Orchestras - Free instruments, coaching, performances
     b) Educational Outreach - School workshops and concerts
     c) Professional Artist Support - Grants, residencies, platforms
     d) Community Concerts - Free public performances
   - Visual: Card-based layout with images

4. IMPACT SECTION (#impact)
   - Title: "Our Impact"
   - Statistics Dashboard:
     - 500+ Students Reached Annually
     - 50+ Community Concerts Per Year
     - 200+ Instruments Provided
     - 30+ Artists Supported
   - Testimonials from beneficiaries
   - Call to action: "Be Part of This Story"

5. NEWSLETTER SECTION
   - Title: "Stay Connected"
   - Email capture form
   - Firebase integration for storage
   - Bilingual support (EN/RO)
   - Success/error messaging

6. FOOTER
   - Navigation links
   - Contact information
   - Social media links
   - Legal links (Privacy Policy, Terms)
   - Copyright notice

=============================================================================
USER JOURNEYS
=============================================================================

Journey 1: Corporate Sponsor Discovery
1. Lands on Hero → Impressed by video and headline
2. Scrolls to Vision → Understands mission alignment
3. Reviews Impact → Sees measurable outcomes
4. Clicks "Contact Us" → Fills out form with organization details
5. Receives confirmation → Sales team follows up

Journey 2: Professional Artist Engagement
1. Lands on Hero → Relates to artistic excellence
2. Navigates to Offerings → Discovers support programs
3. Reviews artist testimonials in Impact section
4. Clicks "Contact Us" → Submits application/inquiry
5. Receives information packet → Applies to program

Journey 3: General Interest / Newsletter
1. Lands on Hero → Curious about organization
2. Explores sections → Learns about mission
3. Scrolls to Newsletter → Subscribes for updates
4. Receives welcome email → Invited to upcoming concert

=============================================================================
TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
=============================================================================

Platform: Next.js 14 (React 18)
Language: TypeScript (strict mode)
Styling: Tailwind CSS (utility-first)
Internationalization: react-i18next (EN/RO)
Database: Firebase Firestore
Authentication: Firebase Auth (custom token)

Performance Requirements:
- Page load: <3 seconds on 4G
- Lighthouse score: >90
- Mobile-optimized
- SEO-friendly

Browser Support:
- Chrome/Edge (last 2 versions)
- Firefox (last 2 versions)
- Safari (last 2 versions)
- Mobile Safari & Chrome
